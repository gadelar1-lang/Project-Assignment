About Me

Hello! Thank you for stopping by.

I’m Gabriel De La Rosa, a retail operations leader who thrives on building strong teams and creating environments where people and businesses succeed together.

Whether it’s scaling omnichannel strategies, mentoring future leaders, or reducing accident costs by over 90%, I bring a people first approach backed by Lean Six Sigma expertise and a passion for operational excellence. Beyond the numbers, I’m proud of the culture I’ve helped shape—raising funds for local causes, supporting community programs, and ensuring every team member feels valued and empowered.
